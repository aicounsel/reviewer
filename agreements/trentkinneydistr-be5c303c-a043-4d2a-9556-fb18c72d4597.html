Distribution Agreement

Preamble

This Distribution Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into as of [Effective Date], by and between Wooden ToyCo, a company organized and existing under the laws of [Wooden ToyCo's Jurisdiction], with its principal place of business located at [Wooden ToyCo's Address] (hereinafter referred to as "Manufacturer"), and BabiesRUs, a company organized and existing under the laws of [BabiesRUs's Jurisdiction], with its principal place of business located at [BabiesRUs's Address] (hereinafter referred to as "Distributor").

WHEREAS, Manufacturer produces and supplies [Description of Products], and Distributor desires to distribute such products; and

WHEREAS, the parties wish to enter into this Agreement to define the terms and conditions under which Distributor will distribute Manufacturer's products.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties hereto agree as follows:

Appointment

This Agreement is made between Wooden ToyCo ("Manufacturer") and BabiesRUs ("Distributor") whereby the Manufacturer appoints the Distributor as its exclusive distributor for the sale of Wooden ToyCo's products within the specified territory subject to the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement. The products covered by this Agreement (the "Products") include, but are not limited to, [List specific product categories or types, e.g., wooden toy cars, wooden building blocks, wooden puzzles, and wooden dollhouses]. The Distributor accepts this appointment and agrees to diligently perform all duties and obligations as set forth herein.

The Distributor shall have the exclusive right to market, sell, and distribute the Manufacturer's products within the territory. This exclusivity is contingent upon the Distributor meeting the performance targets and other conditions as specified in this Agreement. Failure to meet these conditions may result in the termination of this exclusivity as per the terms outlined in the Termination clause of this Agreement.

Term

This Distribution Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of the Effective Date and shall remain in force for a period of five (5) years from the Effective Date, unless earlier terminated in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement. Upon the expiration of the initial term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one (1) year periods unless either party notifies the other party in writing of its intention not to renew at least ninety (90) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

Notwithstanding the foregoing, either the Manufacturer or the Distributor may terminate this Agreement at any time upon providing at least ninety (90) days written notice to the other party if there is a breach of any material provision of this Agreement by the other party that is not cured within thirty (30) days after written notice of such breach is given. Furthermore, either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice to the other party if the other party (i) becomes insolvent or admits in writing its inability to pay its debts as they mature, (ii) makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors, (iii) is dissolved or liquidated, or (iv) has a receiver, trustee, custodian, or similar agent appointed for the major part of its assets.

Territory

The Manufacturer hereby grants the Distributor the exclusive right to distribute the Products within the following territory (the "Territory"): [Specify Territory]. This right is exclusive, meaning that the Manufacturer agrees not to appoint any other distributors or sell the Products directly within the Territory during the term of this Agreement. However, the Manufacturer retains the right to sell the Products directly to customers outside the Territory.

The Distributor agrees to actively promote and sell the Products within the Territory and to not market, sell, or distribute the Products outside the Territory without the prior written consent of the Manufacturer. Any sales made outside the Territory by the Distributor without such consent shall be considered a breach of this Agreement.

This clause does not restrict the sale of the Products through online channels, provided that such sales are in compliance with the territorial restrictions set forth herein and do not directly target customers within the Territory by the Distributor or any other party.

Orders, Prices, and Payment Terms

The Distributor shall submit orders for the Products to the Manufacturer in writing, specifying the quantities of Products ordered and the requested delivery dates. The Manufacturer shall confirm receipt of each order in writing and shall use reasonable efforts to meet the requested delivery dates, subject to availability of the Products and production capacity.

Prices for the Products shall be as set forth in the Price List provided by the Manufacturer to the Distributor. The Manufacturer reserves the right to modify the Price List upon providing thirty (30) days written notice to the Distributor. Any orders placed prior to such notice shall be fulfilled at the prices in effect at the time of the order.

Payment terms shall be net thirty (30) days from the date of invoice, unless otherwise agreed in writing by the parties. The Distributor shall make all payments in the currency specified in the agreement. Late payments shall accrue interest at the rate of 1.5% per month, or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is lower.

Delivery, Title, and Risk of Loss

The Manufacturer shall deliver the Products to the Distributor at the location specified by the Distributor within the Territory. Delivery shall be completed upon the physical transfer of the Products to the Distributor, at which point the risk of loss or damage to the Products shall pass to the Distributor.

The title to the Products shall pass from the Manufacturer to the Distributor upon the completion of delivery and the full payment of the purchase price by the Distributor to the Manufacturer. Until such time, the title to the Products shall remain with the Manufacturer.

Should any Products be damaged or lost while in transit, the Manufacturer is responsible for filing any claims with the carrier, provided that the Distributor notifies the Manufacturer of such damage or loss within a reasonable time frame, not exceeding five (5) business days after the scheduled delivery date. The Manufacturer shall make reasonable efforts to replace the damaged or lost Products or refund the Distributor the purchase price of the damaged or lost Products at the Manufacturer's discretion.

Intellectual Property Rights

The Manufacturer hereby grants to the Distributor a non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable license to use, display, and promote the Manufacturer's trademarks, trade names, and logos (collectively, the "Intellectual Property") solely in connection with the marketing, sale, and distribution of the Products within the Territory. The Distributor agrees to use the Intellectual Property strictly in accordance with the Manufacturer's guidelines and instructions and not to use or register any trademarks, trade names, or domain names that are similar to the Manufacturer's Intellectual Property.

The Distributor acknowledges that all right, title, and interest in the Intellectual Property, including any goodwill associated therewith, shall remain with the Manufacturer, and that any use of the Intellectual Property by the Distributor shall inure to the benefit of the Manufacturer. The Distributor shall notify the Manufacturer promptly of any unauthorized use of the Intellectual Property within the Territory or any infringement claims related to the Intellectual Property. The Distributor agrees to assist the Manufacturer in protecting and enforcing its Intellectual Property rights at the Manufacturer's request and expense.

Upon termination or expiration of this Agreement, the Distributor shall immediately cease all use of the Intellectual Property and return or destroy all materials bearing the Intellectual Property in its possession or control.

Confidentiality

During the term of this Distribution Agreement and for a period of five (5) years thereafter, both the Manufacturer and the Distributor agree to maintain the confidentiality of all confidential information that is exchanged between them or that comes into their possession as a result of this Agreement. Confidential information includes, but is not limited to, trade secrets, business and financial information, and any other information that is treated as confidential by either party. Each party agrees not to disclose any confidential information to any third party without the prior written consent of the other party, except as may be required by law.

Furthermore, both parties agree to take all reasonable precautions to protect the confidentiality of such information and to prevent any unauthorized disclosure. In the event of a breach of this confidentiality clause, the non-breaching party shall have the right to seek all available legal remedies, including injunctive relief and damages.

Notwithstanding the foregoing, information shall not be deemed confidential if it: (i) becomes publicly known through no fault of the receiving party; (ii) is disclosed by the disclosing party to a third party without a duty of confidentiality; (iii) is independently developed by the receiving party without use of the disclosing party's confidential information; or (iv) is rightfully received from a third party without a duty of confidentiality.

Marketing and Advertising

The Distributor shall conduct marketing and advertising for the Products in the Territory in a manner that is consistent with the Manufacturer's brand image and marketing guidelines. The Distributor agrees to use the Intellectual Property solely for the purpose of marketing, selling, and distributing the Products within the Territory and shall not use the Intellectual Property for any other purpose without the prior written consent of the Manufacturer.

All marketing and advertising materials created by the Distributor that use the Intellectual Property must be approved by the Manufacturer prior to their use. The Manufacturer shall not unreasonably withhold or delay such approval. The Distributor shall provide the Manufacturer with copies of all marketing and advertising materials that use the Intellectual Property within ten (10) business days of their creation.

The Distributor agrees to adhere to all applicable laws and regulations relating to advertising and marketing in the Territory. This includes, but is not limited to, laws relating to truth in advertising, the use of endorsements and testimonials, and the prohibition of misleading or deceptive practices.

The Manufacturer reserves the right to review and monitor the Distributor's marketing and advertising activities to ensure compliance with this Agreement and the appropriate use of the Intellectual Property. In the event that the Manufacturer determines that any marketing or advertising activity is not in compliance with this Agreement or is otherwise detrimental to the Manufacturer's brand or Intellectual Property, the Manufacturer may require the Distributor to modify or cease the activity in question.

Sales Targets

The Distributor agrees to use its best efforts to promote, market, and sell the Products within the Territory in accordance with the sales targets set forth below. These targets are designed to ensure a minimum level of effort and success in the distribution of the Products and are subject to review and adjustment by the parties on an annual basis.

In the first year of this Agreement, the Distributor shall achieve a minimum sales target of $100,000 USD.

For each subsequent year, the sales target shall increase by 10% over the previous year's target.

Failure to meet the agreed-upon sales targets for two consecutive years shall constitute a material breach of this Agreement, providing the Manufacturer with the right to terminate this Agreement upon written notice to the Distributor.

The Manufacturer may, at its discretion, provide support to the Distributor in the form of marketing materials, training, or other resources to assist in meeting the sales targets.

Both parties agree to meet quarterly to review sales performance against the targets and to adjust strategies as necessary to achieve the agreed-upon goals.

Reporting

The Distributor shall provide the Manufacturer with written reports on a quarterly basis, detailing the sales of the Products within the Territory. Each report shall include, at a minimum, the following information:

  • Total number of Products sold;
  • Gross revenue generated from the sale of Products;
  • Inventory levels of Products at the end of the reporting period;
  • Marketing activities undertaken and their impact on sales;
  • Customer feedback and any product returns or complaints.

These reports shall be provided within thirty (30) days following the end of each calendar quarter. Failure to provide timely and accurate reports as required under this clause may result in penalties as agreed upon by the parties or, in the absence of such agreement, as reasonably determined by the Manufacturer.

The Distributor agrees that all data and information provided in these reports will be treated as Confidential Information and shall not be disclosed to any third party without the prior written consent of the Manufacturer, except as required by law.

Warranties and Liability

The Manufacturer hereby warrants to the Distributor that at the time of delivery, all products supplied under this Agreement ("Products") will:

  • Be free from defects in materials and workmanship;
  • Be in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations within the Territory;
  • Not infringe on any third party's Intellectual Property rights.

This warranty shall last for a period of twelve (12) months from the date of delivery of the Products to the Distributor. Should any Products be found defective within this period, the Manufacturer agrees to replace or repair the defective Products at its own expense.

The Distributor must notify the Manufacturer in writing of any defects within thirty (30) days of discovery, providing detailed information about the defect and how it was discovered.

The above warranties are in lieu of all other warranties, express or implied, including but not limited to any implied warranties of merchantability or fitness for a particular purpose. The Manufacturer shall not be liable for any indirect, special, consequential, or incidental damages arising out of the use or inability to use the Products.

Nothing in this Agreement shall limit the Manufacturer's liability for death or personal injury caused by its negligence, fraud, or any other liability which cannot be excluded or limited under applicable law.

Indemnification

The Distributor agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Manufacturer, its officers, directors, employees, agents, successors, and assigns from and against any and all losses, damages, liabilities, claims, actions, judgments, settlements, interest, awards, penalties, fines, costs, or expenses of whatever kind, including reasonable attorneys' fees, that are incurred by the Manufacturer, arising out of or resulting from any claim of a third party alleging: (i) that the use of the Intellectual Property by the Distributor within the Territory infringes or misappropriates the intellectual property rights of a third party; (ii) any breach by the Distributor of any representations, warranties, or obligations under this Agreement; or (iii) any negligence or more culpable act or omission (including recklessness or willful misconduct) by the Distributor, or any of its personnel, in connection with the performance of its obligations under this Agreement.

The Manufacturer agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Distributor, its officers, directors, employees, agents, successors, and assigns from and against any and all losses, damages, liabilities, claims, actions, judgments, settlements, interest, awards, penalties, fines, costs, or expenses of whatever kind, including reasonable attorneys' fees, that are incurred by the Distributor, arising out of or resulting from any claim of a third party alleging: (i) any breach by the Manufacturer of any representations, warranties, or obligations under this Agreement; or (ii) any negligence or more culpable act or omission (including recklessness or willful misconduct) by the Manufacturer, or any of its personnel, in connection with the performance of its obligations under this Agreement.

Each party's indemnification obligations as set forth above shall survive the termination or expiration of this Agreement.

Termination

This Distribution Agreement (the "Agreement") may be terminated under the following conditions:

  1. By either the Manufacturer or the Distributor, upon providing at least ninety (90) days written notice to the other party prior to the intended date of termination.
  2. By the Manufacturer, if the Distributor fails to achieve the minimum sales targets as set forth in the Agreement for two consecutive quarters, upon providing thirty (30) days written notice to the Distributor.
  3. By either party, immediately upon written notice to the other party, if the other party breaches any material provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving notice of such breach.
  4. By either party, immediately upon written notice to the other party, if the other party becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or enters into liquidation or dissolution.
  5. Upon termination of this Agreement, the Distributor shall cease to use all Intellectual Property of the Manufacturer, and shall return or destroy all copies of Confidential Information in its possession, custody, or control, except as required to comply with any applicable legal or accounting record keeping requirement.
  6. Any provisions of this Agreement that, by their nature, should survive termination, shall survive termination, including, but not limited to, provisions relating to confidentiality, indemnification, and limitations of liability.

Effect of Termination

Upon termination or expiration of this Distribution Agreement (the "Agreement"), the following provisions shall apply:

  • The Distributor shall immediately cease to represent itself as a distributor of the Manufacturer and shall cease all use of the Manufacturer's Intellectual Property. Any use of the Manufacturer's Intellectual Property by the Distributor after the date of termination shall be considered unauthorized and a violation of the Manufacturer's rights.
  • The Distributor shall, within thirty (30) days of termination, return to the Manufacturer all copies of Confidential Information in the Distributor's possession or control. If return is not feasible, the Distributor shall destroy such Confidential Information and provide the Manufacturer with a written certification of such destruction.
  • All rights granted to the Distributor under this Agreement to distribute the Products within the Territory shall immediately cease, and the Distributor shall return to the Manufacturer any remaining inventory of the Products or, at the Manufacturer's option, dispose of the inventory in a manner directed by the Manufacturer.
  • Any obligations and duties that by their nature should survive the termination of this Agreement, including but not limited to obligations related to confidentiality, indemnification, and intellectual property rights, shall survive such termination.

This clause does not limit any other remedies that may be available to either party under the law or as provided in this Agreement.

Dispute Resolution

This Dispute Resolution clause applies to any dispute, claim, or controversy arising out of or relating to this Distribution Agreement ("Agreement"), including, but not limited to, disputes regarding the interpretation, breach, termination, enforceability, or validity of any provision of this Agreement.

Negotiation: In the event of a dispute, the parties agree to first attempt to resolve the dispute through good faith negotiations within thirty (30) days of written notification of the dispute.

Mediation: If the dispute cannot be resolved through negotiation, the parties agree to attempt to resolve the dispute through mediation under the rules of an agreed-upon mediation service. Each party will bear its own costs in mediation, and the parties will share equally the fees of the mediator.

Arbitration: Should the dispute not be resolved by mediation, the parties agree that the dispute shall be resolved by binding arbitration administered by an agreed-upon arbitration service. The arbitration will be conducted in the English language, and the decision of the arbitrator(s) shall be final and binding on the parties. The costs of arbitration, including the fees of the arbitrator(s), shall be shared equally by the parties, unless the arbitrator(s) decide otherwise based on the merits of the case.

Governing Law: This Agreement and any dispute arising out of or related to it will be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction in which the Manufacturer is incorporated, without giving effect to any choice or conflict of law provision or rule.

Jurisdiction: Any legal suit, action, or proceeding arising out of, or related to, this Agreement or the transactions contemplated hereby shall be instituted exclusively in the federal courts or the courts of the jurisdiction in which the Manufacturer is incorporated. Each party irrevocably submits to the exclusive jurisdiction of such courts in any such suit, action, or proceeding.

Governing Law

This Distribution Agreement (the "Agreement") shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction in which the Manufacturer has its principal place of business, without giving effect to any choice or conflict of law provisions or rules that would cause the application of the laws of any jurisdiction other than that of the Manufacturer's principal place of business. Any legal suit, action, or proceeding arising out of or related to this Agreement or the transactions contemplated hereby shall be instituted exclusively in the federal courts of the United States or the courts of the State in which the Manufacturer's principal place of business is located, and each party irrevocably submits to the exclusive jurisdiction of such courts in any such suit, action, or proceeding.

The Parties agree to comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and ordinances relating to the performance of their obligations under this Agreement. Each Party shall obtain at its own expense any licenses, permits, or approvals required for the performance of its obligations hereunder.
